To track the metrics defined in \ref{sec:evaluation}, the application logs research-specific metadata into a dedicated \texttt{research\_logs} table in Supabase. The complete entity-relationship diagram is shown in Figure~\ref{fig:schema_erd}.

\begin{table}[H]
    \centering
    \begin{tabular}{lll}
        \toprule
        \textbf{Column Name}          & \textbf{Data Type} & \textbf{Purpose}                         \\
        \midrule
        \texttt{log\_id}              & UUID               & Link to specific meal interaction        \\
        \texttt{input\_modality}      & Enum               & voice, photo, text (For RQ2)             \\
        \texttt{processing\_time\_ms} & Int                & Latency measurement ($< 5s$)             \\
        \texttt{agent\_turns\_count}  & Int                & Friction measurement (Intervention Rate) \\
        \texttt{was\_corrected}       & Boolean            & Error correction tracking (RQ3)          \\
        \texttt{confidence\_score}    & Float              & Internal agent confidence metric         \\
        \bottomrule
    \end{tabular}
    \caption{Schema for \texttt{research\_logs} table.}
    \label{tab:research_schema}
\end{table}

        \texttt{carbs}             & Integer (nullable)  & Estimated carbohydrates (grams)                       \\
        \texttt{fats}              & Integer (nullable)  & Estimated fats (grams)                                \\
